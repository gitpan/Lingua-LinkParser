 LI 
LI connects a few verbs like "feel" and "seem" to the preposition 
"like":

           +-LI--+
           |     |
	I feel like a banana

"Like" then makes a J connection to a following noun phrase,
just as it does in ordinary prepositional uses. "Like" thus
has "J+ & (MVp- or Mp- or ... LI-)".
